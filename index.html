How do I import my portfolio — do you connect to my broker?
▾
No — we never connect directly to your broker or ask for your broker credentials. Instead, simply export your holdings as a CSV or Excel file from Zerodha, Groww, Angel One, Upstox, or any other broker, and upload it to the Portfolio X-Ray section. Our system reads your file locally and runs the analysis — your broker login is never involved.
Is the NSE/BSE data truly real-time?
▾
Yes. Pro plan subscribers get live NSE/BSE data with a maximum 15-second delay during market hours. Starter plan subscribers get end-of-day data updated after market close at 3:30 PM IST.
How does the Breakout Radar work?
▾
The Breakout Radar continuously scans all NSE/BSE listed stocks for technical patterns — Cup & Handle, Ascending Triangles, 52-week highs, and volume surges. When a breakout is detected with volume confirmation (typically 1.5x average), an alert is fired with the entry level, target, and stop-loss.
Is Portfolio X-Ray safe to use?
▾
Completely safe. We use read-only data import — we never ask for your broker login credentials. You import a CSV export from your broker or paste holdings manually. We cannot execute any trades on your behalf.
Do you offer plans for wealth managers or HNIs?
▾
Yes. Our Enterprise plan supports multi-portfolio management for wealth managers and family offices, with API access, white-label options, and a dedicated relationship manager. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.
